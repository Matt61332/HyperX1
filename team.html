Chris Hopwood

Chris Hopwood brings a rare combination of industrial operations, emergency response, and public-safety experience to Fire Scientific. After years managing offshore and onshore oil-rig operations — where a single ignition event can cost lives and hundreds of millions in assets — Chris has seen firsthand what legacy fire suppression can and cannot do.

He has served his community as a volunteer firefighter and as a sworn law-enforcement officer, giving him an operational fluency in incident command, mutual aid, AHJ coordination, and the realities of what happens when the tones drop at 2 a.m.

That experience shapes the way Chris represents HyperX 1 to U.S. fire chiefs, safety officers, and industrial buyers. He doesn't sell foam. He translates the technology into the language of the rig floor, the engine bay, and the command post.

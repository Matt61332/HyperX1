Regulatory Risk

"The EPA's PFAS rule is reshaping my foam program. How do I avoid the next round of lawsuits?"

HyperX 1 contains zero PFAS, PFOS, or PFOA — not "short-chain," not "low-content," none. It's fully REACH compliant and WGK 1 water-hazard rated (the safest class), so you're not trading one regulatory problem for another. When your state or insurer tightens the screws in 2027 or 2028, you're already clear.

The bottom line: Switching to HyperX 1 retires your PFAS liability footprint permanently.
UL & NFPA

"My AHJ won't approve anything that isn't UL listed. Where does HyperX 1 stand?"

UL certification is actively in progress, with a target completion of November 2026. HyperX 1 already carries ICAO Level B & C and the full EN (European) suite — the equivalent and in many cases stricter standards — so the UL path is a formalization, not a redesign. Early adopters can lock in pricing and priority delivery today.

The bottom line: Already validated to ICAO and EN standards — UL is the U.S. formalization, not a gating risk.

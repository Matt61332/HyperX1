Why This Matters

UL Certification Unlocks the U.S. Market.

UL certification is the single most important regulatory gateway for fire suppression agents in the United States. It governs AHJ acceptance, insurance underwriting, federal procurement, and NFPA system compliance.

AHJ Acceptance

Fire marshals and local code officials require UL-listed agents for fixed systems, ARFF operations, and high-hazard occupancies.

NFPA Compliance

UL listing is the backbone of NFPA 11, NFPA 25, and NFPA 2001 compliance — the documents your insurer audits against.

Federal & Municipal Procurement

Most federal RFPs, DoD replacement programs, and large municipal contracts require UL listing as a threshold criterion.

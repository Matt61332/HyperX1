The Product

What is HyperX 1?

HyperX 1 is a next-generation wetting-agent foam hybrid engineered for the broadest application range of any extinguishing agent on the market. Manufactured in Germany to exacting European standards and distributed in North America by Fire Scientific LLC, HyperX 1 delivers the fastest cooling effect in the industry — without PFAS, without compromise.

Fastest Cooling

Best-in-class cooling effect, knocking down fires in under 20 seconds.

100% PFAS-Free

REACH and PFOS/PFOA compliant. Biodegradable, non-toxic, WGK 1 rated.

Universal Compatible

Sprinklers, ARFF vehicles, handheld, fixed systems — works with all existing infrastructure.

20-Year Shelf Life

10 years in IBC standard, up to 20 years under optimal storage conditions.
